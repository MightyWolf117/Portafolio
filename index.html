About Me

I'm a passionate backend developer obsessed with clean architecture and scalable systems. By day I build APIs and databases; by night I escape into digital illustration and the furry fandom where creativity has no limits.

My journey started with a fascination for how things work. I dove deep into backend technologies—Node.js, Python, databases, API design—because I wanted to understand the skeleton that powers web applications. The complexity, the elegance, the problem-solving: that's what hooks me.

But there's another side to me. I'm deeply embedded in the furry fandom as a digital artist. This isn't just a hobby; it's a creative outlet where I express myself without the constraints of conventional art world expectations. My illustrations blend character design, vibrant colors, and emotional storytelling. It's raw, it's genuine, and it's mine.

These two worlds collide beautifully. My dev work is meticulous and logical; my art is expressive and chaotic. Together, they define who I am: someone who values both precision and creativity, who refuses to fit into traditional boxes, and who believes that the best work comes from embracing your authentic self.

Beyond code and art, I'm into gaming (particularly narrative-driven games), modding communities, and building tools that empower creators. I thrive in environments where unconventional thinking is celebrated and where personality is welcome.
